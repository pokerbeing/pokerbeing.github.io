2018 World Series of Poker

Main Event Final Table

After surviving a near recordbreaking 7,874 competitors, the 2018 World Series of Poker Main Event Final Table played beginning on July 12. After playing headups for over 10 hours overnight the winner was crowned in the wee hours of Sunday, July 15. Below is the order of finishing.
